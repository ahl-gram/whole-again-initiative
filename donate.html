Why It Matters

Your gift does
one thing

It pays for a woman's areola restoration procedure — in full. We work with a network of paramedical tattoo artists who offer deeply discounted rates to our grant recipients. Every dollar donated is stretched as far as it can go.

Because we operate with minimal overhead, your generosity translates directly into care. No administrative fees, no fundraising salaries, no bureaucracy between your gift and the woman it helps.

$25 Covers artist consultation and intake for one recipient
$50 Covers supplies and aftercare products for a full procedure
$100 Covers half the cost of a unilateral restoration procedure
$250 Funds a full bilateral restoration — completely restoring a survivor
$500+ Sponsors a recipient from application to completed procedure
