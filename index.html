3D Areola Tattoos

More than
skin deep

After mastectomy, many women feel their healing journey is incomplete. 3D areola restoration tattooing is a powerful final step — one that helps women reconnect with their bodies and move forward with confidence.

But although it is not cosmetic, insurance rarely covers it. And the cost falls entirely on the women who need it most. The Whole Again Initiative closes that gap through grant funding, so finances never stand between a survivor and her sense of self.

We partner with skilled paramedical tattoo artists to ensure every woman we serve receives compassionate, expert care — at no cost to her.

The Process

How our grants work

Apply Online

Complete our simple application. We ask about your cancer history, financial situation, and where you are in your recovery. No lengthy forms — we respect your time and privacy.

Get Matched

We pair you with a vetted licensed medical professional near you who is also a tattoo artist and who specializes in areola restoration. Every artist in our network has been reviewed for expertise, positive outcomes, and outstanding patient care.

Heal Fully

We pay your artist directly. You arrive, you receive your procedure, and you leave without a bill. That's it. You've been through enough — this part should be simple.

Grant Eligibility

Who we help for 3D areola tattoos

  • Individuals who have undergone mastectomy or lumpectomy due to breast cancer or genetic mutations
  • Medically cleared by their surgeon to undergo areola tattooing
  • Grant preference given for those who demonstrate financial need
