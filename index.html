Our Mission

More than
skin deep

After mastectomy, many women feel their healing journey is incomplete. 3D areola restoration tattooing is a powerful final step — one that helps women reconnect with their bodies and move forward with confidence.

But it's often considered cosmetic. Insurance rarely covers it. And the cost falls entirely on the women who need it most. The Whole Again Initiative closes that gap through grant funding, so finances never stand between a survivor and her sense of self.

We partner with skilled paramedical tattoo artists across the country to ensure every woman we serve receives compassionate, expert care — at no cost to her.

The Process

How our grants work

Apply Online

Complete our simple application. We ask about your cancer history, financial situation, and where you are in your recovery. No lengthy forms — we respect your time and privacy.

Get Matched

We pair you with a vetted paramedical tattoo artist near you who specializes in areola restoration. Every artist in our network has been personally reviewed for skill, sensitivity, and expertise.

Heal Fully

We pay your artist directly. You arrive, you receive your procedure, and you leave without a bill. That's it. You've been through enough — this part should be simple.

Grant Eligibility

Who we help

  • Women who have undergone mastectomy or lumpectomy for breast cancer
  • U.S. residents with financial need (income-based, flexible criteria)
  • Fully healed surgical sites as confirmed by your medical provider
  • No prior areola restoration procedure within the past 3 years
  • Willingness to share your story (optionally) to help us raise awareness
