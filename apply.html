Apply for a
restoration grant

You've been through enough. This part is simple. We review applications on a rolling basis and respond within two weeks.

Before You Apply

Who we help

  • Women who have undergone mastectomy or lumpectomy for breast cancer
  • U.S. residents with demonstrated financial need (we use flexible, income-based criteria)
  • Fully healed surgical sites, as confirmed by your medical provider
  • No prior areola restoration procedure within the past 3 years
  • Willingness to optionally share your story to help us raise awareness
Not sure if you qualify? Apply anyway — or call us at (800) 555-0192. We'd rather talk it through than have you miss out.

What You'll Need

A few things to have ready

  • Basic contact and location information
  • A brief description of your cancer history and treatment
  • A note from your medical provider confirming surgical healing
  • Basic household income information (self-reported, no documentation required initially)
Your privacy matters. All information you share is kept strictly confidential. We will never share your personal details without your explicit consent.

After You Apply

What happens next

01

We Review

Our team reviews every application personally — no automated rejections. We'll reach out if we have questions. You'll hear from us within two weeks.

02

We Match You

Once approved, we'll connect you with a vetted paramedical tattoo artist in your area who specializes in areola restoration.

03

We Pay Directly

We handle payment directly with the artist. You show up for your appointment — that's it. No invoices, no reimbursements, no out-of-pocket costs.
